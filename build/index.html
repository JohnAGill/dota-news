Play Style

The general idea behind the medic bear build is it use your position as a safelane farming core, to help get you core utility items faster, enabling your team to fight and push more often than the enemy. Aim to get you mekansm and Arcane Boots before 14 minutes game time and begin to group up with your team, start taking towers/fights to help further increase your gold and pushing power. You will not be able to put out a large amount of damage like this, but due to you and your bears naturaly high hp you will be able to stay in the fight longer, allowing for more entangle procs for some damage and a good disable.
